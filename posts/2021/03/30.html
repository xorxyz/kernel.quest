the following types are used:
  • ğŸ‘¼: you. using walk moves you around.
  • ğŸ‘: sheep. you can count them to fall asleep.
  • ğŸ”’ğŸšª: locked door. unlock by entering the right passcode.
  • ğŸ’: gem. reach this to beat the level.
  • ..: floors. this is where you can walk on.
  • , : comma. links two items together.
  • ##: walls. blocks your movement.
  • :N: registers. .
  • ??: slots. takes a single digit hex value.
  • 01: number. a 2 digit hex value. (00-FF)
i added a door and a gem to make the objective visual and clear. the goal is to unlock the door and reach the gem. to unlock the door, you need to fill the register with right values ("to enter the passcode"). you can do the tasks by hand (by editing the lines), or you can use the runtime language to transform the room until it's in a solved state. the later is the intended way, but both technically work.
